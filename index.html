Why I Love Music

Music has been a pillar in my family for as long as i can remember. I honestly can't remember when I heard the first symphony hit my ear, my mom played music to me when I was still in her belly. Everyone in my family has some kind of musical ability. Most are singers, but some play instruments and there's me, a producer and poet. Music has saved my life plenty of times and I'm honestly indebted to music.
